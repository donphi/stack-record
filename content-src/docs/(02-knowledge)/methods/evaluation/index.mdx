## What this map is

Methods for measuring system quality: retrieval metrics, classification metrics, and benchmarking protocols.

## Why this matters

Without rigorous evaluation, improvements are anecdotal. These methods define what "better" means for every pipeline component from extraction to retrieval.

## Where this fits

Evaluation methods draw on [[statistics]] for significance testing and are applied to outputs from [[retrieval]], [[extraction]], and [[experiments]].

## Structure

Notes are split into per-task metrics, end-to-end benchmarks, and human evaluation protocols.

## What this map helps answer

- Which metric best captures retrieval quality for our use case?
- How do we compare two retrieval pipelines fairly?
- When is human evaluation necessary versus automatic metrics?
