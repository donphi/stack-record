## What this map is

Architecture Decision Records (ADRs) currently under evaluation. These are open questions where the team is gathering evidence before committing.

## Why this matters

Pending decisions represent active uncertainty. Making them visible prevents drift, ensures they get resolved, and invites input before choices become irreversible.

## Where this fits

Pending ADRs are promoted to [[accepted]] or [[rejected]] once resolved. They often arise from [[experiments]] results or gaps identified in [[systems]] and [[methods]].

## Structure

Each pending ADR includes: the question, options under consideration, evaluation criteria, and a target resolution date.

## What this map helps answer

- What architectural questions are currently unresolved?
- What evidence is needed to make a decision?
- How long has a decision been pending?
