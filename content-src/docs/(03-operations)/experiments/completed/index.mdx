## What this map is

Experiments that have finished with documented results, whether the outcome was positive, negative, or inconclusive.

## Why this matters

Completed experiments are the evidence base for [[decisions]]. Without accessible results, the same experiments get repeated and insights are lost.

## Where this fits

Completed experiments inform [[accepted]] and [[pending]] decisions. Their methods feed back into [[evaluation]] and [[retrieval]] best practices.

## Structure

Each completed experiment records: hypothesis, method, results, conclusions, and links to any resulting decisions or code changes.

## What this map helps answer

- What did we learn from past experiments?
- Which retrieval configurations were tested and what performed best?
- Where are the raw results and analysis notebooks?
