## What this map is

Architecture Decision Records (ADRs) that were evaluated and deliberately not adopted. These document paths not taken and the reasoning behind those choices.

## Why this matters

Rejected decisions prevent the team from re-exploring dead ends. They preserve the context and evidence that led to a "no" — which is often more valuable than the "yes" decisions.

## Where this fits

Rejected ADRs provide contrast to [[accepted]] decisions. They may be revisited when constraints change and can inform [[pending]] evaluations of similar alternatives.

## Structure

Each rejected ADR captures: the proposal, evaluation criteria, reasons for rejection, and conditions under which it might be reconsidered.

## What this map helps answer

- Why was a particular approach rejected?
- Under what conditions should a rejected decision be revisited?
- What alternatives were considered alongside the accepted choice?
