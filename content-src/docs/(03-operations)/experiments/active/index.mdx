## What this map is

Experiments that are currently running or in progress. Each entry tracks a hypothesis, method, and expected completion.

## Why this matters

Active experiments consume resources and attention. Tracking them here prevents duplication, ensures results are captured, and makes workload visible.

## Where this fits

Active experiments draw on [[methods]] and [[concepts]], use [[pipeline]] and [[runtime]] infrastructure, and produce results that move to [[completed]] or [[failed]] upon conclusion.

## Structure

Each experiment note records: hypothesis, method, data, start date, and expected duration.

## What this map helps answer

- What experiments are currently running?
- What resources are allocated to active experiments?
- When are results expected?
