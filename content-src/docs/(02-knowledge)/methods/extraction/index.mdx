## What this map is

Methods for extracting structured information from unstructured text: entity recognition, relation extraction, and normalisation.

## Why this matters

Extraction is the first step in building a knowledge graph from raw documents. The quality ceiling of every downstream process — retrieval, reasoning, evaluation — is set here.

## Where this fits

Extraction methods consume [[biomedical]] domain knowledge and [[representation_learning]] models. Outputs feed into [[data]] systems and are scored by [[evaluation]] metrics.

## Structure

Notes cover NER approaches, relation extraction, and entity linking/normalisation.
