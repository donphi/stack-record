## What this map is

System notes on data storage and persistence: where extracted knowledge, embeddings, and metadata live at rest.

## Why this matters

Choosing the wrong storage layer creates bottlenecks that are expensive to migrate away from. Getting persistence right early means retrieval, evaluation, and auditing all benefit.

## Where this fits

Data systems receive outputs from [[pipeline]] stages and [[extraction]] methods. They serve [[retrieval]] at query time and are backed up and versioned according to [[reproducibility]] methods.

## Structure

Notes cover relational stores, vector databases, and file-based persistence.

- What storage backend supports similarity search at our scale?
- How do we migrate data when schemas evolve?
